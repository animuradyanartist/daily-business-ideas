# MARKET_MAP

Running atlas of niches, buyers, and comparables studied across all Scout runs. Update existing sections; add new sections only when a run opens a genuinely new niche.

## Async English for non-native software professionals
- Buyer: non-native English-speaking developer, PM, or designer working remote/hybrid for a US/UK/EU company. Reads/listens fine; freezes when typing async. Disposable income (avg remote H1B SWE: $137,695). Most likely Indian, Polish, Brazilian, Turkish, French, Spanish, Ukrainian.
- Where they gather:
  - r/cscareerquestions, r/ExperiencedDevs, r/learnprogramming
  - r/ENGLISH, r/EnglishLearning
  - LinkedIn groups: "Indian Tech Workers in US," "Polish IT Network," "Tech Brazil"
  - Discord servers for international new-grad SWE cohorts
  - TikTok / IG Reels under hashtags like #esl, #businessenglish, #h1b, #softwareengineerlife
- Comparable products + revenue:
  - Talaera (https://www.talaera.com/) — corporate B2B Business English, enterprise pricing. Proves willingness-to-pay; gap is the individual self-serve buyer.
  - English with Maryse (https://englishwithmaryse.com) — solo creator selling email templates to non-native professionals. Proxy proof of solo viability.
  - Effortless English Club (https://effortlessenglishclub.com) — long-running creator with Business Email Templates product line.
  - Easlo (https://www.easlo.co/templates/vocabulary-builder) — Notion-template solo creator at multi-six-figure scale; proves design-led Notion-template flywheel adjacent.
  - Etsy "15,074 English Grammar Worksheets" (https://www.etsy.com/listing/1857603074/) — Star Seller proves bulk-content artifact economics in adjacent ESL niche.
- Price range: $19 (intro) – $39 (individual) – $129 (team-of-5) – $200+/mo (Talaera enterprise).
- Last researched: 2026-04-27
- Status: open

## Adult ESL self-study (general / intermediate plateau)
- Buyer: adult B1–B2 English learner (not professional-tech-specific). Stuck on the "intermediate plateau" — can read/listen fine but cannot speak or write naturally.
- Where they gather: r/EnglishLearning, r/languagelearning, FluentU/Preply blog comments, YouTube comment sections of polyglot creators.
- Comparable products + revenue:
  - Loviet's Notion Vocabulary Tracker (https://www.notion.com/templates/vocabulary-tracker) — top of Notion language category.
  - Kazuki's Vocabulary Book (https://www.notion.com/templates/vocabulary-book-english) — proven seller.
  - Leonardo English (https://www.leonardoenglish.com) — content-led brand around the plateau pain point.
- Price range: $0 (free Notion templates) – $19 (premium templates).
- Last researched: 2026-04-27
- Status: saturated (free vocab trackers dominate; hard to differentiate without a sharper wedge)

## Designer-built Notion templates (general)
- Buyer: knowledge worker / solopreneur looking for productized organization systems.
- Where they gather: Notion marketplace, r/Notion, Twitter design community.
- Comparable products + revenue: Easlo (multi-six-figure solo), Notion Marketplace top creators.
- Price range: $0 – $79.
- Last researched: 2026-04-27
- Status: open as channel, but generic templates are saturated. Wedge must be a domain (language, finance, sales) the designer brings expertise to.
