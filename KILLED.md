## 2026-04-28
- Bilingual LinkedIn personal-brand kit for non-native consultants/coaches (broad) — too generic, founder's design edge weaker without a designer-specific buyer; better wedge is designers-first.
- Pitch deck phrasebook + Canva templates for non-native founders/PMs — presentation-skill muscle differs from writing; Pitch.com / slidebean crowd the design half.
- Visa interview prep deck for US/UK F1/H1B/B2 visa interviews — regulated-industry adjacent, low-LTV (one buyer per visa per lifetime), buyers want certified coaches, not a designer's PDF.
- Cold-DM swipe file for non-native freelance designers (standalone) — best as a $20 add-on/upsell, would cannibalize a bundled offer.
- TikTok/Reels script kit for non-native creators starting English content — founder's design edge under-leveraged (TikTok is text-first); validation harder because conversion happens off-platform.
- Portfolio + case-study Canva templates for non-native designers job-hunting — Behance/Dribbble offer this for free; "non-native" angle weak because portfolios are visual-first.
